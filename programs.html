Workforce Development

Skills Training & Career Pathways

Home-Bridge Foundation's flagship launch program provides industry-recognized certifications and employment pathways in emerging sectors for adults facing barriers to employment in Dillon County.

Industry certification training in construction, green energy, IT, and more
Individualized career coaching and barrier assessment at intake
Direct employer partnerships for job placement in the Pee Dee region
Wraparound support: transportation, childcare referrals, financial coaching
30/60/90-day post-placement follow-up for job retention
Serving adults 18+, prioritizing those below 200% federal poverty level

Affordable Housing

Safe, Quality Housing for Every Family

Stable housing is the foundation of everything else. Home-Bridge Foundation works to develop, preserve, and revitalize affordable housing opportunities for underserved families in Dillon County.

Development of new affordable housing units in underserved communities
Preservation and rehabilitation of existing housing stock
Homeownership education and down payment assistance programs
Housing stability support for families at risk of displacement
Partnerships with HUD, SCSHFDA, and USDA Rural Development

Green Energy & Environment

We advance community-based environmental justice through green energy initiatives and sustainability programs — building economic resilience and environmental health in the communities we serve.

Community-based environmental justice advocacy and education
Sustainability programs advancing environmental health outcomes
Green jobs training connecting residents to emerging energy sectors
Partnerships with EPA, DOE, and USDA Rural Energy programs
