About Home-Bridge Foundation

A community-rooted nonprofit dedicated to building opportunity, housing, and economic resilience in Dillon County, South Carolina.

Our Story

Born From Community, Built for Community

Home-Bridge Foundation was founded by Marcus Hayes, a Dillon County native who saw firsthand the barriers facing families in the Pee Dee region — unstable housing, limited job training, and communities left behind by economic progress.

Incorporated on December 4, 2025, and granted federal 501(c)(3) status, Home-Bridge Foundation is built on the conviction that underserved communities don't lack potential — they lack access. Our job is to build the bridge.

We are headquartered in Latta, South Carolina, and our work is guided by three core programs: affordable housing, green energy & environmental justice, and workforce development.
