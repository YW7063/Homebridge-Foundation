Who We Are

Building Bridges to a Better Future

Home-Bridge Foundation was born from a belief that where you live shouldn't determine what you can achieve. We're a Dillon County nonprofit working to close the gap between underserved communities and the resources, housing, and opportunities they deserve.



Founded by community members who know the Pee Dee region firsthand, we bring local knowledge, deep relationships, and relentless commitment to every program we run.

Home-Bridge Foundation is dedicated to building stronger, more resilient communities by expanding access to safe, affordable housing, advancing green energy, and empowering individuals through workforce development and economic opportunity.

— Our Mission, Home-Bridge Foundation

The Challenge

Why Dillon County Needs Home-Bridge

Dillon County faces some of the steepest economic challenges in South Carolina. We're here because the need is real — and the opportunity to change lives is too.

Top 5
Dillon County ranks among the top 5 highest-poverty counties in South Carolina
Rural
Limited access to workforce training, certifications, and emerging-industry employers
Housing
Critical shortage of safe, affordable housing for working families in the Pee Dee region
Gap
Persistent skills gap between available workers and in-demand green energy and construction jobs
